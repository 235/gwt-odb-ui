Let's explain our service by providing user-view on it:

  • get involved: view what other people have published (text posts, mind-maps) or collected (lifestreams);
  • collect data: get a tool (smart bookmark or browser-plugin), start growing your semantic base - grab and set tags to the chunks of data from sites, links, emails, copy/paste documents, archives; chose datasources for automatic analysis and meshing-up (Google news and docs, Del.icio.us, Yahoo! Mail, RSS-feeds, etc);
  • manage: transform your data to knowledge by viewing and managing it in lifestream (timeline), mind-map (graph), table, and geo-map views; extract what you'd like to with filters (by people, cities, sites, sources); perform on it a search;
  • summarize: keep organized your thoughts around main topic by observing all chunks of it in one place, build your own big picture of the idea. Create a post or mind-map by putting the elements of it together, writing your ideas based on it. Track versions and connections from your post to the original sources;
  • share: publish your topic to the community to solicit ideas and knowledge through comments and new friends, present your results in a blog, or in a report;
  • find out more about the service: get widgets for igoogle, facebook, myspace. Provide custom datasources, building own types of data views and use open project interfaces.
